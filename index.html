About Me

I am a student at Epicodus learning web development and design. I was born and raised in the philadelphia area. I love coding and solving complex problems. I build my own PCs, mostly because I'm an avid gamer. I went to Temple University's Tyler School of Art as a graphic design major for 1 year before I fell in love with coding and went to community college for web development. I did that for another year before disliking that curriculum and working full time in retail. I've since left that job and currently I'm studying at Epicodus for web development.
